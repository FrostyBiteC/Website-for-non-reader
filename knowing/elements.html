Natural Elements

Flame

The hot, bright part of fire that dances and flickers

Candle

Wax with a wick that burns to give light

Campfire

An outdoor fire for warmth and cooking while camping

Volcano

A mountain that can erupt with hot lava and fire

Ocean

The largest bodies of salt water on Earth

River

Flowing water that runs from mountains to the sea

Lake

A large body of still water surrounded by land

Rain

Water falling from clouds to the earth

Waterfall

Water falling from a high place to a lower place

Drop

A small, round piece of water

Wind

Moving air that you can feel but cannot see

Cloud

Fluffy white collections of tiny water droplets in the sky

Sky

The space above us where clouds and birds fly

Breeze

A gentle, light wind that feels nice and cool

Storm

Strong winds with rain, thunder, and lightning

Mountain

Very tall land that rises high into the sky

Hill

A small raised area of land, smaller than a mountain

Valley

Low land between hills or mountains

Cave

A natural hole or hollow space underground

Soil

The top layer of earth where plants grow

Snow

Frozen water that falls as soft white flakes

Glacier

A large river of ice that moves slowly

Ice Cube

Frozen water in a small square shape

Frost

Thin ice crystals that form when it's very cold

Sun

The star that gives us light and warmth every day

Bulb

A glass object that produces light when turned on

Star

Bright points of light in the night sky

Firefly

A small insect that glows in the dark

Rainbow

Beautiful colors in the sky made by sunlight and rain

Stone

A small, hard piece of rock

Pebble

A small, smooth stone found on beaches

Boulder

A very large rock, too heavy to move

Crystal

A beautiful, shiny rock with a special shape

Gem

A precious, beautiful stone used in jewelry
