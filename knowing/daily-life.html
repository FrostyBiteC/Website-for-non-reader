Daily Life

Apple

A sweet, crunchy red fruit that grows on trees

Bread

Soft baked food made from flour, perfect for sandwiches

Rice

Small white grains that are a main food for many people

Milk

White drink from cows that makes bones strong

Vegetables

Healthy plants we eat like carrots, broccoli, and peas

Meat

Food from animals like chicken, beef, and pork

Shirt

Clothing worn on the upper body with sleeves

Pants

Clothing worn on the legs, covering from waist to ankles

Dress

A one-piece garment that covers the body from shoulders down

Shoes

Footwear that protects our feet when walking

Hat

Headwear that protects from sun or keeps warm

Jacket

A warm outer garment worn over other clothes

House

A building where people live with their families

Bed

Furniture for sleeping and resting at night

Chair

Furniture for sitting, with a back and legs

Table

Flat furniture for eating, working, or playing

Door

A movable barrier to enter and exit rooms

Window

An opening with glass to let in light and see outside

Hammer

A tool with a heavy head for hitting nails into wood

Screwdriver

A tool for turning screws to fasten things together

Wrench

A tool for gripping and turning nuts and bolts

Saw

A tool with a blade for cutting through wood

Drill

A power tool that makes holes in materials

Doctor

A person who helps sick people get better

Medicine

Substances that help cure sickness and pain

Hospital

A place where doctors and nurses care for sick people

Exercise

Physical activity that keeps your body strong and healthy

Healthy Food

Nutritious foods like fruits and vegetables for a strong body
