Concepts

Red

A bright color like apples, roses, and stop signs

Blue

A cool color like the sky, ocean, and blueberries

Green

A natural color like grass, leaves, and frogs

Yellow

A sunny color like bananas, lemons, and the sun

Orange

A warm color like oranges, pumpkins, and carrots

Purple

A royal color like grapes, violets, and eggplants

Pink

A soft color like flowers, cotton candy, and flamingos

Circle

A round shape with no corners, like a wheel or ball

Square

A shape with four equal sides and four corners

Triangle

A shape with three sides and three corners

Rectangle

A shape with four sides, longer than it is wide

Star

A pointed shape with five or more points

Heart

A shape that represents love and caring

Zero

The number 0 - nothing, empty, or the start of counting

One

The number 1 - a single item, the first number

Two

The number 2 - a pair, like two eyes or hands

Three

The number 3 - a small group, like three little pigs

Four

The number 4 - like four legs of a table or dog

Five

The number 5 - fingers on one hand

Six

The number 6 - half a dozen eggs

Seven

The number 7 - days in a week, colors in a rainbow

Eight

The number 8 - legs on a spider

Nine

The number 9 - players on a baseball team

Happy

Feeling joy and pleasure, like when you smile

Sad

Feeling unhappy or sorrowful, when tears may come

Angry

Feeling mad or upset when something is wrong

Surprised

Feeling shocked when something unexpected happens

Scared

Feeling afraid or frightened of something

Excited

Feeling eager and enthusiastic about something fun

Clock

A device that shows what time it is

Calendar

Shows the days, weeks, and months of the year

Day

The time when the sun is shining and it's light outside

Night

The time when it's dark and the moon comes out

Morning

The start of the day when the sun rises

Evening

The end of the day when the sun sets
